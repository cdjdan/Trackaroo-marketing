What is Trackaroo?

A single platform that brings ticketing and (optional) live bus tracking together — built for UK coach and school transport operators.

Live bus tracking & route replay

Parents see the bus on a live map. Operators can replay routes and review performance.

Mobile tickets & QR passes

Replace paper with digital passes. Drivers scan quickly with the Trackaroo driver app.

Operator-controlled portal

Manage routes, students, passes and reporting from a modern browser-based portal.
