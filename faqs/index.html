Frequently asked questions

Quick answers and pointers — if you want specifics for your operation, contact us and we’ll respond quickly.

Is Trackaroo designed for UK operators?

Yes — Trackaroo is designed for UK coach and school transport operators running school services.

Can parents track the bus?

Yes — parents can view live vehicle locations (where enabled) and receive disruption updates.

Does Trackaroo support digital bus passes?

Yes — Trackaroo supports QR passes and driver scanning via the driver app.

Can Trackaroo replace paper tickets?

In most cases, yes — the platform is built around digital passes and operator-controlled records.

Does Trackaroo integrate with existing systems?

Trackaroo can support multiple tracking sources, including integrated tracking feeds. Tell us what you use and we’ll advise the best approach.

Is Trackaroo just a tracking system?

No — Trackaroo combines live tracking (optional) with ticketing, scanning, notifications and operator management tools.

Is Trackaroo suitable for small operators?

Yes — it’s designed to work whether you run a single route or manage a larger fleet.

How do I get started?

Book a demo and we’ll walk through your routes, current tracking setup and ticketing needs.

Questions about PCI / payments / security?

We’re happy to share the relevant details for your setup. Email info@trackaroo.co.uk and tell us what payment methods you need.
